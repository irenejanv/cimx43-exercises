Veggie Sandwich

There once was a veggie sandwich
Who was told his whole life,
He wouldn’t amount to much.
“You’re full of veggies!” they said.
“No meat!”
Defined by what he was missing—

Instant defeat.

“Don’t listen to them,” the cucumber said.
“Your ingredients are intentional between the bread.”

A thick creamy tzitziki on a Kaiser roll
Topped with vegan tendies, breaded and crisp!
Leaving the eater nothing to miss!

Next a layer of homemade cilantro mayo
Then, cucumber and a slice of tomato…

Topped off with arugula and shaved parmesan cheese.
Cilantro mayo again, a big squeeze!

The bread on top, keeps everything stacked tight.
Veggie sandwich, a real delight.
Despite the naysayers, he’s the best of his kind.
We’ll have him again, one more time!

By Irene Vailikit
